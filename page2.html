ATTRACTIONS

There are some famous places to visit recommended for you

Bygone

In the village of St Marychurch close to Babbacombe, Bygones is open every day except Christmas Day, and in the summer open till 10pm in the week. Shop as our grandparents used to shop, go into a Victorian parlour,visit the illuminated Fantasy Land in miniature, see the scenic working model railway and climb into the cab of a real steam loco. Walk through a World War I trench.

Babbacome Theatre

A Visit To Babbacombe Is Not Complete Without A Visit To BABBACOMBE THEATRE - 600 seat theatre - Superb Variety Show Running From 10th February until 21st October Tuesday & Wednesday Evenings From 8:15 p.m. 2015 Show "STARLIGHT"

Cookington Village

Open through the year to Christmas Eve, and with free admission, you can see how things used to be. Ride there from the sea front in a horse and carriage. Visit the stately Manor House, stroll through the gardens, watch the village blacksmith at work, eat at the Mill Restaurant with its working water wheel. An unchanged village from o Britain's most popular resorts.

Oddicombe Cliff Railway

It's well worth going down to Oddicombe beach, but it's quite a climb back up to Babbacombe. Try the Cliff Railway.

SPORTS

The variety of sports is sure to make you pleasure

Sailing CLub

If you don't own your own boat (yet!) we have a number of club dinghies for hire. We also have a growing kayak section if that's what you are looking for. We offer a range of RYA accredited dinghy training for adults and juniors with our own qualified Instructors as well as Power Boat and sailing theory courses. Off the water we have a wide ranging social programme all year round and we look forward to seeing you soon.

DeepSea Fishing

Four pages cram packed full of useful information if you want to take up shore fishing for the first time. With photos and detailed guides you'll find this pages perfect if you're coming down to Torbay to fish. If you're new to fishing you'll need to know how to tie the knots and make your own rigs! This is the place for you.

Kite Surfing & Kite Buggying

Feel the wind blowing in your face. Clear skies. Beautiful waters. A kick of adrenaline pumping through you.Those are just the top of the icing to the cake as to why you must try kitesurfing in Exmouth View Hotel

Horse Racing

Nestling in a valley at the edge of historic Newton Abbot, the racecourse is impeccably presented, with friendly staff and a smiling face. The food and drink is the stuff of legend, with South West suppliers and delicious local produce, a day at Newton Abbot is a delight for young and old.
